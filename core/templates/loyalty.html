How it works

Instant rewards
  1. Book and complete your stay — earn {{ earn_rate|default:5 }} points automatically.
  2. Once you reach {{ redeem_cost|default:35 }} points, you are eligible to redeem.
  3. During booking, select "Use points" to unlock a {{ discount_percent|default:10 }}% discount.
  4. We deduct {{ redeem_cost|default:35 }} points at redemption and award new points after checkout.
Points never expire while your account stays active.

FAQ

Points are awarded automatically when a reservation is marked as completed (checked-out).

When booking, select the "Use points" option in the room modal. If you have {{ redeem_cost|default:35 }} points or more, we apply a {{ discount_percent|default:10 }}% discount and deduct {{ redeem_cost|default:35 }} points.

Points do not expire while your account remains active. If your account is inactive for an extended period, points may be reviewed.
