FAQ

How long do orders take?

Most dishes are delivered within 20-30 minutes. We will notify you if an item needs longer.

How are charges applied?

All items are charged to your active room reservation. You will see them on your folio.

Can I edit or cancel a request?

Contact reception if you need to adjust or cancel. We will do our best if the order has not been prepared.
