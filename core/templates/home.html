Welcome to Great Irish Hotels

Experience the very best of Ireland with Great Irish Hotels. Our award-winning collection provides a warm, welcoming retreat in every region, from the Wild Atlantic Way to the heart of Ireland. Whether you desire luxury, tradition, or adventure, our family-run hotels offer the perfect base to discover Ireland’s charm, vibrant culture, and breathtaking natural beauty.

Each of our hotels is steeped in local heritage and offers a unique blend of modern comfort and traditional Irish hospitality. Enjoy gourmet dining experiences crafted from the freshest local ingredients, unwind in luxurious rooms with stunning views, and take part in exclusive activities that celebrate the spirit of Ireland. Our dedicated staff are passionate about making your stay memorable, whether you’re planning a romantic getaway, a family adventure, or a business retreat. With Great Irish Hotels, you’ll find not just a place to stay, but a place to belong—where every guest is welcomed as part of our extended family. Start your journey with us and create unforgettable memories in the heart of Ireland.
